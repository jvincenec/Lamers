The Kylos take reduced damage from Plague. Kylo Ren (Unmasked)'s leadership can heal the Plague, if zeta'd. Nightsisters have no anti-crit mechanics and no way to stop the turn meter train.

Strategy: How to Beat Old Republic (Carth L) with First Order (KRU L)

Kylo Ren (Unmasked) and Kylo Ren benefit from the damage over times. First Order Officer can give tenacity up to himself and another character, which also helps with damage over time. Dispel Zaalbar's taunt, and just attack a bunch to get the turn meter train rolling. Stop the damage over time from spreading, dispel Zaalbar frequently from his taunt to focus on attackers/support first. Can't rely on debuffs/have really high potency (Bastila Shan (Fallen) and Carth will have 80% tenacity, rest 30%). Bypass the crit avoidance (Zaalbar+Mission have 55% Crit Avoidance, rest 30%). Use their slow speed against them with turn meter gain, you should have speed advantage. Kill order: Canderous Ordo, Carth Onasi or Mission Vao.
