There are three models of activity:
  • Optimum assumes that everybody deploys and you have reasonable success with the missions. It is not necessarily a lower bound -- the missions are just estimates of how well you do, but it is an optimistic view. You might score a star or two higher, especially if you are close on some sections. You won't make it if participation is low.
  • 90% is a more pessimistic view: it assumes that 90% of your gp is deployed and that missions are only 90% as effective as the estimate. Not a lower bound, but this should realisticly be possible.
  • Decreasing is in between. It assumes full participation and good performance on the early phases and tapers off on the later phases (this matches what I observe).
  • Wat Shards is the same as Decreasing but it tries to force the TB to reach 3N to obtain Wat shards.
Contact @gorgatron#3094 to tailor these assumptions.


See also https://jetkokos.ru.
